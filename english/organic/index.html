Organic

Recently, we have received more inquiries not only from Japan but also from overseas customers. To meet international standards such as EU and US FDA, in addition to Organic JAS certification, we have started working towards HACCP certification with the support of the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries and JETRO (Japan External Trade Organization).

At our farm, we conduct annual pesticide residue inspections at domestic testing centers. Considering our export situation and future prospects, we also had our tea tested at SGS, a world-leading inspection agency in Geneva, Switzerland. The results have arrived, and we have cleared all pesticide standards set by SGS!
