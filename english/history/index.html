History and Tradition

In 1969, we began exploring pesticide- and chemical-free farming in response to a customer's simple yet powerful request:
“I want to drink tea that's free from pesticides.”
The first few years were extremely challenging. We struggled with pests and disease, and our harvests dropped to just one-third of what they had been. Through much trial and error, we finally began to see hope by 1975.
Thanks to the support of many people, our dedication to pioneering organic cultivation was recognized. We have since received numerous awards, including the prestigious Minister of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Award.
