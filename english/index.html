About Us

In 1954, a tea field was created from scratch on the bare mountaintop at an altitude of 360 meters, and we began living in a six-tatami room on the mountaintop. In 1969, to response to a customer's request to drink "Organic tea", we began producing tea without the use of pesticides or chemical fertilizers.
